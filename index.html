My Life journey

  • 9th Mar,2003

    My Birth time

    My birth was a highly anticipated moment for my parents .My parents on the day I was born . Were very happy to meet me they felt so happy to be blessed with a baby girl as I was the first girl in my family. They felt so proud of me and as time passed by , they supported and helped give me a bright future

  • March 2005

    My First Day to School

    The first day when dad took me to my primary school, I was very excited and a little worried about how a new place, new friends, and teachers would be. However, my fears did not last long and I easily adjusted myself. I saw a lot of new faces in the classroom. They also welcomed me and became my friends in a short time.We had a total of five classes on the first day at school. In the first class, the teachers introduced us and told us funny stories instead of teaching books. I liked all of them very much. My first day at school was the biggest event and the brightest memory of my life. This is the day I took the first step towards a beautiful life

  • My Dad My inspiration

    My dad has been both my father and my friend.My dad is the most influential person in my life because he always pushes me to do my best no matter what decision I may have; he provides me with the information I need to succeed;he always has a positive outlook on life, no matter what the situation may be. My father strives to push me to achieve well in anything that I decide to do.
